01

Research interest

My work explores dynamic fluctuations of current and mass, and the power spectra they generate, across a wide range of non-equilibrium systems — systems that violate detailed balance in the bulk, carry nonzero spatial correlations, and often obey more than one conservation law. The central question I keep returning to: how do conservation laws shape the dynamics of systems driven away from equilibrium?
